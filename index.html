Particle Weather

Real-time conditions visualised through a p5.js particle system. Particle colour, movement, and behaviour adapt to live atmospheric data.

Built with vanilla JavaScript and p5.js, this project fetches live weather data from the Open-Meteo API and maps it to a generative particle system. 800 particles orbit the canvas centre — their speed driven by wind, their colour palette shifting across six weather states: rain, wind, snow, cold, humid, and hot.
